============================
Muddle 3 - labels everywhere
============================

Branch muddle3_labels, for development to make everything pass labels, instead
of the individual parts of labels.

Original branch at/after revision 361.

::

  mkdir -p ~/sw/m3
  cd ~/sw/m3
  svn checkout https://muddle.googlecode.com/svn/branches/muddle3_labels --username tibs@tibsnjoan.co.uk

Defining the "m3" command::

  alias m3='python /Users/tibs/sw/m3/muddle3_labels/muddle/muddled/'

Creating the example::

  mkdir -p ~/sw/m3/eg
  cd ~/sw/m3/eg
  m3 init svn+http://muddle.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/muddle/examples/gnu_package builds/01.py
